The Infrastructure for Agency AI Visibility

Digital marketing is changing. For decades, the goal was Search Engine Optimization. In the era of ChatGPT and Perplexity, the goal is AI Optimization.

Aseon is the data infrastructure built for SEO, Marketing, and PR agencies. We provide the tools and reporting your agency needs to turn the "hidden" AI algorithm into a scalable, high-value service for your clients.

The "Black Box" Problem

Before Aseon, agencies were flying blind. Clients are asking to be "visible in ChatGPT", but until now, there was no way to measure success, prove ROI, or determine a strategy.

The AI algorithm is not one-size-fits-all. Every industry and every client faces a different source footprint. Without hard data, agencies are just guessing.

Aseon removes the guesswork. We provide the thermometer (the data) so your agency can provide the medicine (the work).
