The Standard for LLM Intelligence

The internet has evolved. For decades, the goal of digital business was to be found. In the era of Generative AI, the goal is to be cited.

Aseon is the data intelligence platform built for this new reality. We bridge the gap between corporate data and Large Language Models (LLMs), giving businesses the insights they need to understand how AI perceives, ranks, and recommends their brand.

The "Black Box" Problem

Before Aseon, companies were flying blind. While marketing teams have sophisticated tools for SEO and social media, they have had zero visibility into the world’s most powerful information gatekeepers: models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

When a potential customer asks an AI for a software recommendation, a product comparison, or a brand review, does the model mention you? Does it recommend your competitor? And crucially—what data is it using to make that decision?

Aseon removes the guesswork. We transform the "black box" of AI into a transparent, measurable dashboard.

The Aseon Platform

We don't just provide data; we provide control. Our technology is designed to help enterprises audit and optimize their presence in the generative web.

  • Visibility Tracking: Real-time monitoring of brand mentions and citation frequency across all major foundation models.
  • Competitive Intelligence: A deep-dive analysis into why an AI might prefer your competitor's solution over yours, backed by semantic data.
  • Prompt Engineering Analysis: We simulate thousands of user interactions—from simple queries to complex, multi-turn conversations—to stress-test how your brand holds up under different contexts.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Actionable metrics that allow you to refine your public documentation and content strategy to align with how LLMs retrieve information.
